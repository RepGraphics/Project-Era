ARKLANDPVP
A Discord bot for an ARK: Survival Evolved unofficial Cluster.

This bot was made in collaboration with Loading Production all Discord Side & Verification related content are developed by us "Project Era",
 whilst the ARK:SE Client was developed by Loading Productions

ARKLANDPVP Features

Numerous commands are available to use, below you can view the commands list:

Admin Commands

These commands are only usable by ARKLANDPVP Admins!

  • Admin (Add & Remove)
  • Alpha Points (Award & Deduct)
  • Award (Earned & Purchased Points)
  • In-Game: Broadcast
  • Nitrado (Access & Modify Nitrado API)
  •  Remove (Earned & Purchased Points)
  • Reset (Point & Player Databases)
  • Wipe Settings (Set Wipe Dates)
  •  

Player Commands

These commands are usable by anyone!

  • Alpha Points (view)
  • ARKLANDPVP (Server Query & Stats)
  • Give (Earned Points)
  • Help (View Help/Guides)
  • Leaderboard (Earned Points)
  • Profile (View Player Profiles)
  • Wipe (View Wipe Info)
